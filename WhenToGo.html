Looking at the table above, we can divide Toronto's climate into three:

Summer:

  • With high temperatures, in this period, the city is crowded of residents walking around and visitors.
  • There are many festivals and events, and the beaches are open.
  • June-August is the most popular time to visit!

Fall/Spring:

  • The weather is warm, you can do all the outdoor exploration you want.
  • Fall festivals, such as the Toronto International Film Festival, are known to draw a sizable audience.

Winter:

  • Winters in Toronto are cold, windy, and snowy.
  • Flights are usually cheaper!
  • There are some activities as skating, festive Christmas Market, Toronto Light Festival and more.
